What is Vintage product?

It is an object that represents a previous era or time period in human society. An item should be at least 100 years old to be defined as an antique. Generally speaking if the item is no older than an antique but not less than 20 years, it falls under the term vintage.

How does trade work?

Register/Add your vintage product with us and trade other vintage product anytime. You can trade any listed product in categories which matches your product value. On approval of both side of trade product is then exchange with the respective client.
